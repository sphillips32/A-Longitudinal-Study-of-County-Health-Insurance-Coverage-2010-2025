# County Health Insurance Coverage Study (2010–2025)

A panel data study examining how county-level health insurance coverage changed across the United States over 15 years, and which socioeconomic conditions move alongside it.

> **Data source:** [County Health Rankings & Roadmaps](https://www.countyhealthrankings.org) (Robert Wood Johnson Foundation & University of Wisconsin Population Health Institute)

---

## Overview

This project uses 16 annual releases of CHR&R data to track uninsured rates at the county level from 2010 through 2025. The study is **descriptive** — it characterizes patterns and associations (including the ACA Medicaid expansion period ~2014) without making causal claims.

**What this project produces:**
- A clean, county-year panel dataset spanning 2010–2025
- Trend analysis and descriptive statistics at the county and state level
- Regression models (OLS, Ridge, Lasso, Random Forest, Two-Way Fixed Effects)
- Trajectory clustering and quantile regression for subgroup heterogeneity
- Reproducible notebooks organized by analysis stage
